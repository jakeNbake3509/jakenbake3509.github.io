Tips for creating good survey questions

There are many things when it comes to survey questions and creating the questions without a few guidelines will cause issues. That is why here at Survey4you we offer a set of rules to help guide you with what to ask.

Tips for surveying customers

  • Have a main question that you want answered

  • It may seem smart to have a lot of different topics asked in one survey, but surveys that do so are rarely finished. Be specific in exactly what type of survey you are wanting and make a goal. If you are wanting to know why you are losing customers, then it would be best to make a churn survey.

  • Remember to be unbiased and precise

  • Surveys with questions that are long, complex, or vague will cause many issues when it comes to getting feedback. So, it is recommended that the questions are simple and written in easily understood vocabulary.

  • Being precise is critical as it makes the questions smaller and helps with coherency of the questions.

Tips for surveying employees

  • Keep the survey anonymous

  • You will get much more honesty and participation if the employees know they won't be traced back to what they are answering.

  • Don't have too long of a survey

  • It is a good idea to keep the survey as short as possible. Not only will you have less participation, but also more automatic answers that aren't going to be good for improving the company's overall successfulness.
