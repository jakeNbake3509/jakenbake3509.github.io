FAQ

How is feedback received?

We will directly link a phone number or email to receive all the feedback from the consumers. The moment someone submits a completed survey, you or an employee will receive those answers to review. Along with this, there will be a file set up that combines all submitted data to show the overall rating of every opinion in stars.

What information do we need?

Our main priority is ease of use and simplicity for making surveys. All we need is your chosen plan, specified survey questions and the preferred method to receiving the completed surveys. With this information, you will be receiving your feedback in no more than a couple days after published.

Wondering what plan to chose?

Our first plan is the basic plan which post the surveys on websites through ads. Second plan is the combo package which post the surveys on websites as ads and also sends them out as emails. Third plan is the deluxe package which post the surveys on websites as ads, emails them to people and will text them to people. Prices are negotiable

Specifiactions of surveys

We let you make the survey to your liking and wants. If you want it 1 minute or 20 minutes long that is ok. However, it can be difficult to know what questions to ask and how to make a successful survey. Therefore, we offer a complete guide on how to ask the questions whether it be a survey for customers or employees.
